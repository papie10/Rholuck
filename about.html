Who We Are

Rholuck Service Nigeria Limited was incorporated under the provision of the Companies and Allied Matters Decree (1990) in Nigeria on the 6th August, 2010. Rholuck Services Nigeria limited is an indigenous firm involved in occupational health, environmental and quality training, manpower development consultancy and environmental protection services in Nigeria. We are a team of health, safety, environmental and quality management Professionals.
We are members of the American Society of Safety Engineers (ASSE), Institute of Safety Professionals of Nigeria (ISPON), Institute of Chartered Chemists of Nigeria (ICCON), Chemical Society of Nigeria (CSN), and Association of First Aiders (AoFA) – UK.

Our Mission & Values

  • Mission: To be a world class Quality, Occupational Health, Safety and Environmental Consultancy firm.
  • Vision: To provide world class Quality, Occupational Health, Safety & Environmental services to our Clients.
  • Core Values: Safety, Professionalism, Passion for Excellence, Integrity, and Leadership at all levels.

Meet Our Team

We are a team of certified professionals: environmental scientists, engineers, HSE experts, and trainers. Our staff hold certifications from FMEnv, NESREA, DPR, NOSDRA, ITF, NIPEX, and more.

Our Accreditations

Rholuck Services Nigeria Ltd is an accredited environmental consultancy firm authorized to prepare:

  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
  • Environmental Assessment (EA)
  • Environmental Auditing and Reports (EAR)

We are recognized and accredited by the following national and state-level authorities:

  1. Federal Ministry of Environment (FMEnv)
  2. National Environmental Standards & Regulations Enforcement Agency (NESREA)
  3. National Oil Spill Detection and Response Agency (NOSDRA)
  4. Department of Petroleum Resources (now NUPRC)
  5. Delta State Environmental Protection Agency (DELSEPA)
  6. Ogun State Environmental Protection Agency (OGEPA)
  7. Lagos State Environmental Protection Agency (LASEPA)
  8. Niger State Environmental Protection Agency (NISEPA)
  9. Bayelsa State Ministry of Environment
  10. Rivers State Ministry of Environment
  11. Oyo State Ministry of Environment
  12. Industrial Training Fund (ITF)
  13. Nigerian Petroleum Exchange (NIPEX)
  14. Centre for Management Development (CMD)

We are also certified to provide HSE (Health, Safety & Environment) training by various government agencies, including: FMEnv, NESREA, NOSDRA, DPR, NIPEX, CMD, LASEPA, OGEPA, and the Oil and Gas Trainers Association of Nigeria (OGTAN).
